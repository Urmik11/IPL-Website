THALA IS BACK
MS DHONI
The legendary wicketkeeper-batter is set to reprise his role for the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) after getting retained by them as an 'uncapped player'. Prior to the IPL 2024 season, Dhoni relinquished his captaincy responsibility in favour of rising star Ruturaj Gaikwad. Dhoni is all set to play for Chennai Super Kings in the IPL 2025, Popularly called "Thala" by CSK fans.
TATA IPL 2025
RCB CAPTAIN
VIRAT KOHLI
Virat Kohli is the captain of Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) for IPL 2025. After stepping down in 2021, Kohli has returned to lead the team, as confirmed by RCB legend AB de Villiers. Kohli brings vast experience, having previously guided RCB to the IPL 2016 final, aiming to end their title drought this season.
TATA IPL 2025
COSTLY RETENTION
KLAASEN
Heinrich Klaasen was the most expensive retention in the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2025, retained by Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) for ₹23 crore. This was more than the ₹21 crore retained by Virat Kohli of Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB).
TATA IPL 2025
T20WC WINNER
ROHIT SHARMA
Rohit Sharma continues to be a Mumbai Indians player, being announced as one of the players retained by the franchise for IPL 2025. Rohit Sharma will continue to represent the Mumbai Indians, a team he has become synonymous with over the last 10 years and many successes in the Indian Premier League.
